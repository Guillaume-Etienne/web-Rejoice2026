The virtuous circle

The Rejoice principle

A simple idea, a rare alchemy.

1

You travel

You join a small-group restorative retreat — La Poumerole (Auvergne), Nepal, Norway… A space to breathe, connect, come back to yourself.

2

You come back transformed

Authentic encounters, practical tools, surprises. You leave revitalized, with a new inner compass.

3

Children get supported

Part of your retreat directly funds our actions: schools, training centers, orphanages in India and Nepal.

"By restoring yourself here, you help children there.
Your well-being becomes their chance: that's the Rejoice principle!
And that's why we don't look like any other charity!"
