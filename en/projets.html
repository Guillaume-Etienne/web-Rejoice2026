Our approach

How Rejoice acts, concretely

1

We meet

No commitment without an in-person visit. Before any funding, we meet the organisation and its beneficiaries on the ground.

2

We fund

Direct payment to the local partner, no commercial intermediary. Every euro is tracked: receipt, supporting documents, and field photos.

3

We follow

Regular field visits, ongoing exchanges with partners, and reporting on our transparency page.
