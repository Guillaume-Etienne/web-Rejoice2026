✦ Introduction video

A former elite freestyle snowboarder turned head of a family SME of around forty people, it was after years of seeking meaning in my various ventures that in 2019 a turning point came in my life. The time for "others" arrived, and I steered my new projects towards discovery, hospitality, sharing, and humanitarian aid.

Over recent years I have made several trips to sensitive countries such as India and Nepal. I needed to be on the ground with the NGOs, understand their needs, and appreciate the remarkable work being done there.

I therefore decided to found the association REJOICE in 2024 to provide financial support to NGOs abroad. What drove my choice was the extreme urgency in which some of these organisations find themselves, fighting a level of deprivation that is hard to imagine.

"This is my commitment: to be in the field as the manager and guarantor of every action that flows from your generosity."

— Laurent Perraud, President of Rejoice

Our commitment

Uncompromising transparency

Detailed accounts, outsourced accounting, open books. You give to an association that hides nothing.

📊

100% to the field on average over 2024–2025

Over the 2024 and 2025 financial years, almost all donations received went directly to projects. A small "safety reserve" is kept in the bank to absorb unforeseen exchange rate or logistics costs.

📑

Accounting by OnlyCompta

Our accounts are kept by OnlyCompta, an independent accounting firm. Every entry and exit is documented, dated, and auditable.
