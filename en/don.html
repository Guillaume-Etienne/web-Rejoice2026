For individuals

As a non-profit of general interest (loi 1901), donations to Rejoice entitle you to:

66% income tax reduction (up to 20% of taxable income). In practice: if you give 100 €, you recover 66 € on your tax bill — the donation only costs you 34 €.

75% IFI reduction if you are liable for the French Wealth Tax (up to €50,000 reduction). 100 € given = 25 € remaining out of pocket.

For companies (corporate philanthropy — art. 238 bis CGI)

Companies benefit from a 60% tax reduction on the donation amount, up to €20,000 OR 0.5% of pre-tax revenue — whichever is more favourable.

In practice: a €1,000 donation to Rejoice costs the company only €400 after the reduction. For SMEs, the 0.5% of pre-tax revenue cap often allows going well beyond €20,000. Any excess beyond the cap can be carried forward over the next 5 financial years.

The tax receipt is generated automatically by HelloAsso after each donation, and sent to your inbox.
