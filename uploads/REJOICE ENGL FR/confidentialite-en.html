Privacy Policy

At Rejoice, we collect as little data as possible — only what we need to reply to your messages, process donations and handle retreat applications.

01Data controller

The data controller for your personal data is Rejoice, a French non-profit (loi 1901 association) registered in France.

  • Contact: contact@rejoice.ngo
  • Website: rejoice.ngo

The association is not required to appoint a Data Protection Officer (DPO) under article 37 of the GDPR. Any question about your data can be sent directly to the address above.

02Data we collect

We only collect data you actively share with us. No data is collected without your knowledge.

Contact / retreat application form

Our retreat enquiry form uses your own email client (mailto:) — no data is stored on our servers. The message reaches us only when you press send from your inbox.

Newsletter

If you subscribe to our newsletter, we keep only your email address to send you updates. Unsubscribe in 1 click from any email.

Donations

Donations are processed exclusively by HelloAsso (see section 3). Rejoice does not collect or store any banking data.

03HelloAsso donations — embedded form (iframe)

💛 Important noticeUpdate

Since May 2026, the HelloAsso donation form is embedded directly within our site as an iframe. This integration improves your browsing experience but loads third-party content from HelloAsso servers when you open the donation window.

Who collects your data during a donation?

The entire donation flow is handled by HelloAsso (HelloAsso SAS, 79 rue La Boétie, 75008 Paris, France), acting as an independent data controller for payment operations and tax-receipt generation. Rejoice only receives donation notifications (amount, donor identity, optional message) and never receives your banking data.

Data processed by HelloAsso during a donation

DataPurpose
Name, email, postal addressTax receipt issuance & donation confirmation
Donation amount, frequency (one-off / monthly)Payment processing & transfer to Rejoice
Banking data (card / IBAN)Payment execution — encrypted & processed by HelloAsso's payment provider. Rejoice never has access.
IP address, technical dataPayment security, fraud prevention

Cookies set by the HelloAsso iframe

When you open the donation window on our site, the HelloAsso iframe may set third-party cookies on your device — only after you interact with it (clicking a "Donate" button). These cookies are necessary for payment processing and transaction security.

As long as you do not open the donation window, no HelloAsso content is loaded and no third-party cookie is set.

05Recipients

Your data is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties for commercial purposes. It may only be shared with the following recipients:

  • Authorized members of Rejoice — to handle your requests
  • HelloAsso — for donation processing (see section 3)
  • Our website host — for technical site delivery
  • Public authorities — only on lawful request

06Retention periods

  • Contact requests: 3 years after our last exchange
  • Newsletter subscriptions: until you unsubscribe
  • Donation records (Rejoice): 6 years (accounting & tax obligations — French art. L. 102 B LPF)
  • Payment data (HelloAsso): per HelloAsso's policy & applicable legal periods

07Cookies & trackers

The site rejoice.ngo sets no analytics or advertising cookies. No marketing tracking tool (Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, etc.) is integrated.

The only third-party cookies that may be set come from the HelloAsso iframe, and only after you have opened the donation window (see section 3).

YouTube and Vimeo videos, when you click to play them, may also set cookies — managed by their respective publishers.

08Your rights

Under articles 15–22 of the GDPR, you have the following rights over your data:

  • Right of access — get a copy of data about you
  • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, write to contact@rejoice.ngo. We respond within 30 days.

For data processed by HelloAsso (donations), you may exercise your rights directly with HelloAsso: helloasso.com/confidentialite ↗

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the French data-protection authority CNIL: cnil.fr ↗

09Security & transfers outside the EU

Rejoice.ngo is served over HTTPS. Communication with HelloAsso is also HTTPS, and payments use standard banking protocols (3D Secure).

Your data is not transferred outside the European Union by Rejoice. For transfers possibly performed by HelloAsso or video hosts (YouTube / Vimeo), please refer to their respective policies.
